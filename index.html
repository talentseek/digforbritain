Earn Money

Earn a living wage whilst making a difference, support your local community and rebuild the economy.

No previous experience is required and full training is provided by the farm.
Anyone can apply provided you are fit, healthy and 18+.
Hours vary from site to site, a usual shift is from early morning to mid afternoon.
Contract lengths vary depending on location and crop.
You will be working outside picking fruit such as strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, blueberries and cherries.
All roles will meet the national living wage.
Simply sign-up, we will contact you to place you in suitable positions locally.
